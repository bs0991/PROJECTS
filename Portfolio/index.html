A Little Bit About Myself

Like most young adults in college, I struggled in deciding on what career to pursue. Around 20 days out from graduating college I knew two things - 1.) I needed a job and 2.) My Criminal Justice degree didn't help me stand out much. Luckily, Uncle Sam is always hiring!

Little did I know that the next 8 years spent on active duty in the United States Army would be some of the most rewarding and challenging experiences of my life. I've served in a variety units in different locations to include Explosive Ordnance Disposal - aka bomb squad.

Fast forward to today and I'm now an Army Veteran currently pursuing a career as a software developer. As soon as I wrote my first line of code I knew that I had found a new passion. My hope is that I find an organization who is just as passionate about serving others as they are about learning and creativity.

Oh yeah and I'm obsessed with Batman and watches.
